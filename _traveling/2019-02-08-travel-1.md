## The Best Ski and Outdoor Destination in Japan
So many Japanese style spa hotel allows you to enjoy snow and spa outdoors in -20 ℃. Thay have impeccable ski resorts, beautiful volcanic lakes, and sightseeing railways along the coastline. Taking an icebreaker in the Pacific to see ambushes or trudging through the snow in the forest to search for deer is also a unique experience.
